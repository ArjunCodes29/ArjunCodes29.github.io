Man decided not to steal my scooter

Date: December 17, 2024

Today as I was locking my scooter outside the gym, a man approached me. I thought he was going to ask me for money or sell me something, instead he offered me advice...

He told me to tighten the noose of my lock, and that if I didn't he could pull the scooter right out. He was a bike/scooter thief. Blowing smoke into my face, he told me a little bit about his world: how easy it was to steal bikes; how some scooters were high tech and would have wheels that spun backwards when stolen; how he could cut through most locks in under 60 seconds. He seemd to take pride in how good he was at his job.

He told me to get a different lock that would work, for a second I thought "ahh so this was a ploy to sell me something", but he never gave me a link or the name of the lock that would be harder to cut. He told me that he was actually going to steal my scooter, but for some reason decided against it, he said "you looked like a good man". But he was also confused why he told me this, and why he just didnt go ahead and steal my scooter. I think it was some mix of his conscience and wanting to show off. But he will still continue to steal bikes like before.

After he was done he got told off or "got shit for it" by his gang. They lost an easy target. He then cycled away. I too scootied away, I did not want to leave my bike there anymore. I'll go to the gym later.
