What is a Digital Garden?

A digital garden is a collection of interconnected notes, ideas, and insights that grow and evolve over time. Unlike traditional blogs, digital gardens are not organized chronologically but rather by themes and connections between ideas. They represent a more natural way of organizing and sharing knowledge.
